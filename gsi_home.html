About
Greystone Institute

Welcome to Greystone Institute, a renowned Brisbane-based higher education provider dedicated to shaping future leaders and empowering minds.

At Greystone Institute, we believe that education is not confined to the classroom; we offer a dynamic and immersive journey designed to prepare you for the complexities of the ever-evolving global business arena. As the business landscape continues to rapidly change, our programs are designed to equip you with the skills, knowledge, and adaptability you need to thrive in an increasingly interconnected and dynamic world.

Why Choose
Greystone Institute

In today's fast-paced and increasingly complex business environment, there is a critical need for a new approach to business education. The traditional models of teaching and learning, which often rely heavily on passive instruction and standardized curricula, no longer adequately prepare students to excel in the dynamic, global, and technology-driven world of business. Therefore, the Bachelor of Business seeks to address these challenges by embodying the following principles.

Bachelor of Business Snapshot

Total Program Length:

6 Trimesters completed over 3 years. Students complete 2 Trimesters each year.

Number of Units

Students will complete 24 units focused on unique topics and key business skills sets. Students complete 4 units per Trimester.

Weekly Class Hours and Study Commitment:

Each week, you will attend 12 hours of scheduled class, on campus. In addition, you should expect to spend 28 hours per week on personal study, which could include time for studying, completing assignments, participating in team meetings, and more.
